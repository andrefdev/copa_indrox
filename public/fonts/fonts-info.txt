Download and place the following fonts in this directory:

1. BebasNeue-Regular.woff2
   - Download from: https://fonts.google.com/download?family=Bebas%20Neue
   - Use the Regular (400) weight version

2. DMSans-Variable.woff2
   - Download from: https://fonts.google.com/download?family=DM%20Sans
   - Use the Variable font that includes all weights (100-900)

After downloading, rename the files to match the names above exactly.
